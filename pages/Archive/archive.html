The Black Bar Codex


The Black Bar Codex is a collection of free media websites. The Codex contains clean and safe sites, including verified platforms to get free games, films, television and music


I. Movies and Television

  • Cineby.app
    A website providing unlimited free streaming for almost every movie, television show and documentaries.
  • Xprime.tv
    The second best alternative to Cineby, providing much of the same content, it's almost a clone. A very reliable back-up.
  • AnimeKai.me
    The best site for streaming any and all Anime. Features hard subs and high quality dubs in many languages.
  • BestSeries
    The Cineby of Television. A free streaming platform dedicated and specialising in Television series.

II. Gaming

  • GOG Games
    One of the best safe websites to get free games easily. Slow download speeds are unavoidable but it remains a reliable and safe place to get free games.
  • Steamrip
    A safe place to get steam ripped games. A very pleasing user interface combined with a lack of adverts make it easy to use.
  • AnkerGames
    A personal favorite, AnkerGames stands out because it has suprisingly stable and confident download speeds, mirrors and sources for the games.

III. Music and Podcasts

  • Youtube Music Desktop
    This open source desktop app is a free unofficial music streaming service that utilises Youtube and the almost infinite library of music to essentially frankenstein a really slick music experience with a built in ad-blocker.
  • Pocketcasts
    A free streaming site offering up a wide array of podcasts aggregated from many other paid sites. Some podcasts may be absent from this, simply due to copyright, but most of the time you can find 95% of what you would find elsewhere.
