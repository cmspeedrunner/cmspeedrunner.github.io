About

The Black Bar Archive is a collection of sources for open, free and unlocked media.

BBA is completely non commercial and free to use. No ads, just well organized, verified material that has been vetted and honed down to the usable sources.

Core Values

  • Free and open access to digital media and information.
  • Only verified, clearly sourced, and trustworthy material is shared.
  • Nothing on the site will ever cost money to access.
  • Everyone deserves access to knowledge, no restrictions, no barriers.

FAQ

Motive?

The Black Bar Archive exists because access to information is fading at an alarming rate. The Ivory tower that is information is becoming more and more guarded, whether that be locked behind paywalls, a victim of the new online safety bullshit or simply unaccessable for certain people, centralizing these open sources is an act of digital preservation, resistance to obscurity, and an assertion that transparency should not be rare.

Put simply, information is not a privilege, it is a right.


Where does the content come from?

The sources are vetted and verified rigorously before ever reaching the site. For every 1 media source on the Black Bar Archive, 100 were discarded at the faintest hint of obscure ethics, suspicious web activity or a general mistrust in the sites operations.
